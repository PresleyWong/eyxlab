Who We Are

What’s in a name? For us — everything.

EyxLab (pronounced "eye-ex lab") is a name born from the fusion of vision, technology, and experimentation. The prefix “Eyx” is intentionally abstract — hinting at “eye” for clarity and insight, and “x” for the unknown, the future, the experimental. Coupled with “Lab”, it reflects our identity as a place where bold ideas are tested, refined, and brought to life.

We chose a name that’s short, memorable, and completely our own — just like the products and solutions we build.
